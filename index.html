Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

Clicking on any of the four crystals below will add points to your score. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The values of each crystal will change when the game starts again.
